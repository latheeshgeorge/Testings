<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">VITRO S1 BEAN TO CUP MACHINE</span></h2>
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The brand new Coffetek Vitro S1 Espresso has a very compact footprint but delivers great coffee for its size. With a clean and stylish black glass finish, the Vitro is built around already proven and reliable technology. With one bean hopper and two instant canisters it offers a wide selection of your favourite fresh bean barista style drinks.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Its versatile tank fed water supply means you can use it without the need to connect it to the mains, but the tank fed model can easily be converted to mains supply at a later date if needed. There are lots of options such as base cabinets and payment validators.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">A perfect high quality drinks machine, fit for any break-out area, small kitchenette or bar/restaurant.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong><span data-mce-mark="1">8 Speciality Drinks With A Single Touch</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The eight selection touch screen button menu offers Espresso, Americano, Cappuccino, Caf&eacute; Latte, Chocolate, Cafe Mocha and hot water for tea.</span></p>
