<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Expobar Monroc Coffee Machine</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"></span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Monroc is an efficient espresso machine that fulfils all demands for a nice espresso in a simple and easy way. The user-friendly functions of the machine are based on well-tried technology and by skilful and professional craftsmen.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">This means that Monroc is a reliable and solid machine that you can really trust, every day &ndash; year after year. Monroc is very suitable for smaller caf&eacute;s and restaurants where you wish to serve espresso, cappuccino, caff&eacute; latte or other espresso based drinks.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Monroc is a two-group espresso machine with automatic water filling and a steam tank with a 11.5 litre capacity. Monroc has an electronic cup dosage in order to easily be able to pre-programme the required amount of water for various cup sizes.</span></p>
