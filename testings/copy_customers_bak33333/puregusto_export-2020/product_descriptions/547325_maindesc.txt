<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>CHERRY SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>

<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Rocky Road Hot Chocolate</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">3 pumps Sweetbird Chocolate Sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Cherry Syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Toasted Marshmallow syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the sauce with the syrups and add the steamed milk.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Top tip!&nbsp;Sprinkle with cherry, biscuit or chocolate pieces and mini-marshmallows!</span></p>
