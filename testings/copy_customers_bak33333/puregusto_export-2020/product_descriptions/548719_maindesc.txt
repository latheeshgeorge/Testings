<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Birchall Single Estate - Earl Grey Prism Loose Leaf 250g</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><br />Birchall Earl Grey is a scented pure origin tea grown exclusively at Rwanda&rsquo;s leading tea estate.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><img style="float: right;" src="https://www.puregusto.co.uk/images/www.puregusto.co.uk/category_thumb/024436Birchall-Earl-Grey-Loose.jpg" alt="Medium" /></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Our Virunga Earl Grey is a scented pure origin tea, sourced from the finest tea gardens in Rwanda, found close to the country&rsquo;s border with the Democratic Republic of Congo and the Virunga National Park. In this region, the tea bushes grow slowly, at the high altitude and relatively low temperatures, developing greater flavour before they are individually picked.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Our pursuit of quality ensures that all our leaves are only ever hand plucked. The leaf, bud and tender stem are carefully pinched together in a &lsquo;perfect pick&rsquo; and subsequently blended with a traditional bergamot oil to give a distinctive flavour of citrus, with spicy floral notes. It really is perfection to a tea and we look forward to you enjoying it as much as we do.</span></p>
<p><br /><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Birchall&nbsp;consider their signature black teas to be truly special and unique in the tea world and we&rsquo;d like to tell you why. Our black teas are all sourced exclusively from the finest tea estates across East Africa, from Rwanda through to the Great Rift Valley in Kenya; this is generally considered to be where the very best teas are to be found.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">This equatorial region&rsquo;s altitude, high rainfall and rich volcanic soil provide the ideal conditions that are integral to growing tea of unparalleled flavour and taste. It really is perfection to a tea and we look forward to you enjoying it as much as we do.</span></p>
