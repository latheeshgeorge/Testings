<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">London Fog</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Vanilla syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Earl Grey Tea</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Prepare the Earl Grey tea according to the manufacturer&rsquo;s instructions, then add the syrup and a splash of steamed milk.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Flavoured Latte</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double shot of espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps of Sweetbird syrup (choose from the coffee flavours)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pull&nbsp;a double shot of espresso to your cup, add 2 pumps of your chosen syrup and top up with steamed milk.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Top tip!&nbsp;Vanilla, Caramel and Hazelnut are classic partners for coffee, so start with these. You could also try Amaretto, Butterscotch or Gingerbread for something a little bit different.</span></p>
