<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>HAZELNUT SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Secret Squirrel</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Amaretto syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Hazelnut syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the espresso with the Amaretto syrup. Steam the milk with the Hazelnut syrup and add to the espresso.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hazelnut &amp; Caramel Latte</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Hazelnut syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the sauce, syrup and espresso in a cup, top with the milk</span></p>
