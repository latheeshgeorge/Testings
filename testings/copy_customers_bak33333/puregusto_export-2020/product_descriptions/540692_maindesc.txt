<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>Gaggia Anima Prestige</strong></span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><em><strong><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Highlights and Features</span></strong></em></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>INTEGRATED MILK CARAFE</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Froths the milk for an incredibly dense, consistent and hot result thanks to its unique two frothing chambers. In a few seconds, you can enjoy your perfect Cappuccino or latte macchiato: your coffee is brewed and the milk carafe froths the milk all at the touch of a button. It delivers a splash-free stream of velvety milk straight into your cup. With an automatic cleaning cycle and the ability to be removed and stored in the fridge, it is as hygienic as it is efficient.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">ONE-TOUCH BEVERAGE SELECTION</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">You can prepare your favourite coffee and milk specialties at one touch of a button, thanks to the integrated milk carafe and multi-beverage interface.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">AUTO-CAPPUCCINO SYSTEM</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Thanks to the fully integrated double chamber milk carafe, having a perfect cappuccino or a frothed milk, directly in the cup, only takes seconds with just one touch of a button. The milk carafe is cleaned after every use with the unique auto-clean carafe that you can activate at one touch if you wish, after each milk preparation.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">REMOVABLE PATENTED BREWING GROUP</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">It is the heart of the machine. It replicates the actions of a barista in an automatic mechanism. An ideal dose of coffee is tamped into a neatly compressed puck and it allows hot water to run through the puck at a suitable pressure for a great coffee.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">OPTIAROMA</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">It is the heart of the machine. It replicates the actions of a barista in an automatic mechanism. An ideal dose of coffee is tamped into a neatly compressed puck and it allows hot water to run through the puck at a suitable pressure for a great coffee.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">100% CERAMIC GRINDER</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Anima is equipped with a robust 100% ceramic grinder that turns beans into a fine powder and extracts maximum flavours, without ever tasting burnt. It has 5 different settings of grind that grant you the finest grind for a full-bodied espresso or a coarser grind for a lighter tasting coffee.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">PROGRAMMABLE DISPENSING SYSTEM</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Each drink can be fully customized and you can store your preferred settings per coffee and milk beverages for future brews, such as strength, temperature and length.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">15-BAR PUMP</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pumps the water through the ground coffee at high pressure to ensure better-tasting crema topped espressos.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">GROUND COFFEE OPTION</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">This allows you to brew a different blend of coffee without changing the beans in the dedicated container but using the ground coffee program.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">PROGRAMMABLE PRE-INFUSION</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Few seconds of pause between the first shower of hot water and the brewing process moisturises the coffee grounds, extracting the full bouquet of aromas, giving your Espresso a unique body and taste.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">GAGGIA ADAPTING SYSTEM</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The innovative technology of the Gaggia Adapting System adjusts the grinding process, making sure the selected quantity of ground coffee is used for your espresso, whatever blend you choose. Just like a professional barista.</span></p>
