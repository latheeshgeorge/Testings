<h1><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Salted Caramel Iced Chai Latte</span></h1>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Ingredients:</span></h3>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 level scoops of Simply Chai Latte powder</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">300ml Semi Skimmed Milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps of Salted Caramel Syrup (Simply Or Monin)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">5-6 Ice cubes</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Whipped Cream</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Cinnamon Dusting</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Method:</span></h3>
<ol>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pour the milk into a blender.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Add the Chai Latte powder, Salted Caramel syrup &amp; ice cubes.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Blend for 30 seconds.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Serve in a tall glass.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Finish with whipped cream,&nbsp;cinnamon dusting, and a straw.</span></li>
</ol>
