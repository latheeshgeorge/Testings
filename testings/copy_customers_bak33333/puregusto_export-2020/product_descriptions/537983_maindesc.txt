<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></h2>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">PureGusto Cioccolato Suprema is a recipe inspired by spanish drinking chocolates that are taking the nation by storm, as with coffee, consumers are more and more aware of the quality of what it is they are actually drinking, furthermore they're discerning palates are looking to more premium and rich flavours than those standard chocolate drinks from an age ago.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">With this in mind years ago our MD developed this rich, luxurious hot chocolate drink, still produced to the exact original recipe, this luxury chocolate drink is a true delight every single cup, first sip to the last...</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">At a whopping 25% pure cocoa content its not shy on the chocolate either! why not top with quality whipped cream, chocolate flakes or even our deliciously fluffy mini marshmallows to finish every cup off amazingly.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>DIRECTIONS</strong> - Swirl 25g of cioccolato Suprema drinking chocolate into 200ml of milk - Heat using steam arm on your coffee machine until your desired temperature is reached, Make it thicken more by increasing the temperature further.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Have a blender... make a super frothy thick chocolate, simply follow the mixing instructions above and blend for around 20 seconds for a really bubbly chocolate drink.&nbsp;</span></p>
