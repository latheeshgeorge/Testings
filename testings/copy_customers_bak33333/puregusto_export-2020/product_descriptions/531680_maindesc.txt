<h2 class="product_title entry-title"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">WMF 1500S BEAN TO CUP MACHINE</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The WMF 1500S is a medium volume super automatic coffee machine, which produces real espresso-based speciality coffee beverages, at the press of a button. It is recommended for a daily volume of 180-200 beverages and features all of WMFs industry leading technologies and more.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">A versatile unit with modular componentry, the 1500S excels in a multitude of environments. Whether it is an up market hotel bar, the QSR / Convenience environment or the corporate office block the WMF 1500s is the machine for your needs.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Based on the NEW GENERATION-Platform, the WMF 1500S coffee machine is the successor of previous top-seller coffee machine &ndash; the WMF Presto! As the subsequent model, the WMF 1500S coffee machine includes all of the great qualities of the previous models and improves upon them in many areas. The new design comes with a touch screen and optional EASY MILK-System for hot milk, hot milk foam and also cold milk, opens up entirely new opportunities for coffee specialities and top-quality chocolate.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">With the Dynamic Milk System add-on, preparing the latest trendy specialities such as cold milk foam beverages, is just as much child&rsquo;s play as creating the classic latte macchiato or cappuccino. Above all, the WMF1500S puts the yummy factor into gastronomy.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1"><strong>THE 1500S FEATURES</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The WMF Easy Milk system gives you unparalleled milk foam and temperature consistency.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The WMF Plug&amp;Clean fully automated and HACCP certified milk cleaning system.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">A brandable 8&Prime; touch display for individualised menu settings, instructional cleaning animations.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Independent brewing parameters for each coffee hopper.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Self aware system with customisable visual alerts and animations displayed to demonstrate solutions.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Self service functions with a locked menu</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Data backup and updates through a USB stick</span></li>
</ul>
