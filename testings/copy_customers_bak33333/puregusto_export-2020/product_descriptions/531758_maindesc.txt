<h2><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">WMF 5000S COFFEE MACHINE</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The WMF 5000S is a high volume super automatic coffee machine, which produces real espresso-based specialty coffee beverages, at the press of a button. It is recommended for a daily volume of 400+ beverages and features all of WMFs industry leading technologies and more.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The 5000S now features the new WMF Dynamic Milk system, which gives both hot and cold milk foam and several different foam types. Whether it is a busy conference centre, an </span>up market<span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"> hotel, the QSR environment or a busy office block the WMF 5000S is the versatile high volume coffee machine for your needs.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The WMF 5000S is our speciality machine for those who live life in the fast lane. It perfectly satisfies the need to provide the fullest range of indulgence and finest quality at maximum speed. Whenever and wherever you don't have time to wait for coffee specialities.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Consequently, the WMF5000S has an inbuilt recipe for success: The more indulgence created per hour, the greater your daily turnover. And that, you can count on.</span></p>
