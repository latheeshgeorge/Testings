<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Is convection roasting really better quality or are we just blowing hot air? (excuse the pun)?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>Convection roasting</strong> or air roasting is exactly how it sounds, smooth streams of hot air gently pass over the coffee beans until perfectly roasted. This creates a smooth and equally as important, an even roast, without any scorching or over-roasting usually associated with traditional drum roasters.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">There are only a handful of commercial convection roasters in Europe, you&rsquo;ve found one! Welcome to exceptionally smooth roasted coffee.</span></p>
