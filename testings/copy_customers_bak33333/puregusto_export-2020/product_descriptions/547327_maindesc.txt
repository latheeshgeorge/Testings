<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Strawberry Lime Hot Fruit Soothie&trade;</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">50 ml Sweetbird Strawberry&nbsp;Smoothie</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Lime&nbsp;Syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hot Water</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pour smoothie into cup and add syrup. Top with hot water, stir and serve.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Mojo Mojito</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">3 pumps Sweetbird Lime syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 shots white rum</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">8-10 fresh mint leaves</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">soda water</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">crushed ice</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Muddle the mint with the lime syrup in a highball glass. Fill the glass with ice and add the rum. Stir and top with the water.&nbsp;Garnish with a sprig of mint and a couple of lime slices.</span></p>
