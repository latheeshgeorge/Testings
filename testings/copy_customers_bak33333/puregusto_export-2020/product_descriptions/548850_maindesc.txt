<h3 id="pagelayout_0_pagetype_0_pagecontent_0_tabcontent_0_repTabContentBlock_conContentBlock_2_genTitle_2" class="title">Milk and coffee &ndash; a perfect duet</h3>
<p>The GIGA 5 is the first automatic coffee machine from JURA designed to prepare the perfect caff&egrave; latte. Its secret lies in the optimum mix of milk and coffee during preparation. The only way to achieve this is for the milk and coffee to flow into the cup at the same time. The GIGA 5 can prepare coffee and heat up milk simultaneously, a feature previously restricted to professional machines in the most elegant bars and restaurants. Finally, the coffee is topped with a crown of milk foam.</p>
