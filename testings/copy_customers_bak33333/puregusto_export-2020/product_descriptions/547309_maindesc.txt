<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>FRENCH VANILLA SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>

<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">French Vanilla Nut Cappucino</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird French Vanilla syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Hazelnut syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">double espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">foamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine espresso and syrup. Top with milk and dust with brown sugar. Now that&rsquo;s a cappuccino!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Cherry Danish</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Eggnog syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Cherry syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird French Vanilla syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">whipped cream</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine the eggnog syrup and 1 pump of the cherry syrup with the steamed milk. Finish with a dollop of cream flavoured with the french Vanilla syrup and drizzle the remaining cherry syrup over. Naughty.</span></p>
