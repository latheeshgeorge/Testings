<h1 style="text-align: center;">Bravilor Mondo Twin</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Bravilor Mondo twin machine is a quick filter machine and is designed for locations without a water connection. This coffee machine omes with 4 hot plates and 2 brewing stations. It has a water indicator which tells the customer whether the machine has been filled or not. It also signals when the coffee is ready and also when it needs descaling to help maintenance of the machine.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Key Product Features:</p>
<ul>
<li>Hand fill, no need to plug into mains</li>
<li>Indicators when coffee is ready and also when the machine needs descaling</li>
<li>4 x 3 pints of coffee (48 cups at a time)</li>
</ul>
