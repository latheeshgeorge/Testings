<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>JURA IMPRESSA Z8 COFFEE MACHINE</strong></span></h1>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The Z8 from JURA boasts an unbeatable standard of coffee. For long coffees, the world&rsquo;s first automatic speciality coffee machine for the home with one-touch lungo function uses a special preparation method to additionally combine the coffee with hot water during the extraction process, resulting in full-bodied, deliciously light specialities.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.&reg;) facilitates the superb development of flavour for ristretto and espresso, with even trend specialities always a success thanks to fine foam technology and the automatic switching from milk to milk foam. </span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Operation and the selection of one of the 21 different specialities takes place intuitively, thanks to self-explanatory graphics on a high-resolution 4.3" touchscreen colour display. If desired, Smart Connect even enables the machine to be controlled via the free JURA Coffee App. And thanks to RFID technology, the CLARIS water filter is detected automatically.</span></p>

<h3 id="pagelayout_0_pagetype_0_pagecontent_0_tabcontent_0_repTabContentBlock_conContentBlock_2_genTitle_2" class="title"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">P.E.P.&reg; and fine foam technology for unrivalled coffee quality</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">State-of-the-art Swiss technologies ensure top barista-style quality. The AromaG3&nbsp;grinder is now twice as fast, yet preserves all the delicious aroma of the coffee be-ans. The Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.&reg;) ensures the optimum extraction time by pulsing the hot water through the ground coffee at the exact frequency for the amount of water to create a short speciality, such as a ristretto or espresso. Even on-trend creations with milk and milk foam are exceptionally easy to produce by simply touching the display.&nbsp;</span></p>
