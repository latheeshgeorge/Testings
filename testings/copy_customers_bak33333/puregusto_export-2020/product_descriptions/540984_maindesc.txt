<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Birchall Earl Grey Tea is a unique, masterful blend, closely guarded by the fifth generation of Birchall Graham&rsquo;s family. Our teas are sourced entirely from estates across East Africa, from Rwanda through to the Great Rift Valley in Kenya, where the very best teas are to be found.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Our pursuit of quality ensures that all our leaves are only ever hand plucked.
