<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Easter Chick Surprise</span></h3>
<div>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">If your customers have little monkeys with them this Easter, they&rsquo;ll love this eggstra special treat!</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Banana syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">whipped cream</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine sauce and syrup, add the milk, top with whipped cream and decorate with chocolate sauce on the outside and caramel sauce in the middle &ndash; like a creme egg!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Chunky Monkey</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Banana syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 scoop Chocolate frapp&eacute;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">ice</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">professional blender</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Fill glass with ice, top with milk and pour into the blender jar. Add the scoop of frapp&eacute; powder and syrup and&nbsp;blend till smooth.&nbsp;Pour back into the cup and serve.</span></p>
