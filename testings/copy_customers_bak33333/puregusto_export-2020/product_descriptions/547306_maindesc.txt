<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>COCONUT SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>

<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Co-co-cookies Happ</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Coconut&nbsp;syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1/2 a 40g scoop Zuma Cookies &amp; Cream frapp&eacute; powder</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine your frapp&eacute; powder and syrup in your cup, add a splash of hot water and stir to make a paste. Top up with steamed milk, now enjoy!</span></p>
