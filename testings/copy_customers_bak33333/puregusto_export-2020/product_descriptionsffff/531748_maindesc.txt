<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;">Matrix Mini Monarch</span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;">The Mini Monarch 3 has been specially designed to meet the needs of outlets where this is a demand for quality beverages, but where space is at a premium. The Mini 3 is smaller than most pour over machines and offers a choice of eight beverages and hot water as standard. </span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;"> The Mini Monarch's small size allows for it to be installed almost anywhere. The choice of mains or a pump water system mean that the machine can be re-sited with the minimum of effort. </span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;"> Drinks: Cappuccino, Espresso, Caf&eacute; Latt&eacute;, White Coffee, Black Coffee, Chocolate, Chocomilk, Mocha and Hot Water for Tea. </span><br /><br /><span style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: small;"> Height 660mm, Width 165mm, Depth 455mm , Weight 19Kg.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
