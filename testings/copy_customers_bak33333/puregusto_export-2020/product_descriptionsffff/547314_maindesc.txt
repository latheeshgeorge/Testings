<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>MINT SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Minty Mocha</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Mint syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Chocolate syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">double espresso</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine espresso and syrups in a cup and top with the milk.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Peppermint Punch</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Mint syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">4 pumps Sweetbird Chilli syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 scoop Zuma Vanilla frappe powder</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">ice</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">professional blender</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Fill cup with ice, add syrups, top up with milk and pour into blender jar. Add frappe powder and blend until smooth. Pour back into cup and serve.</span></p>
