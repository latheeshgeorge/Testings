<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>ORANGE SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Peach &amp; Orange Hot Fruit Soothie&trade;</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">50 ml Sweetbird Peach Smoothie</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Orange Syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hot Water</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pour smoothie into cup and add syrup. Top with hot water, stir and serve.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Iced Chocolate Orange</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">3 pumps Sweetbird Orange syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">3 pumps Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 shots vodka</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">ice</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Shake the ingredients with the ice in a cocktail shaker, strain and serve in a martini glass. Garnish with a twist of orange peel.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Top tip!&nbsp;Top with cream for extra decadence.</span></p>
