<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Berry Bakewell Soothie</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Amaretto&nbsp;syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">50ml Sweetbird Raspberry &amp; Blackcurrant smoothie</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hot water</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine smoothie&nbsp;and syrup in your cup, top up with hot water and stir. Now enjoy!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Caramel Truffle Latte</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Amaretto syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">shot of espresso</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine espresso, sauce and syrup. Top with milk and drizzle with a little extra sauce.</span></p>
