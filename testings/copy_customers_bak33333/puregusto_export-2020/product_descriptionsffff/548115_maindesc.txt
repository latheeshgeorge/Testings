<h1 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">TWININGS BLACKCURRANT &amp; BLUEBERRY TEA</span></strong></h1>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">What does it taste like?</span><br /><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Bursting with the mouth-watering flavour of blackcurrants and blueberries, this infusion will take you back to your childhood.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">How do I drink it?</span><br /><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Using one bag per person, pour on boiling water and infuse for two to three minutes. Leave for a little longer if you prefer a stronger taste.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Ingredients</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hibiscus, Rosehips, Blackberry leaves*, Natural Blackcurrant and Blueberry Flavourings With Other Natural Flavourings (7%), Liquorice Root*</span></p>
<div class="content-layout__item">
<div class="free-content"><br /><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">*All our herbs are gently steamed, the process is gentle to protect their delicate taste.</span></div>
