<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Trick or Treat</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Pumpkin Spice syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine syrups with espresso and top up with steamed milk. Serve with your scariest latte art, of course!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Chocolate Caramel Bliss</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Dark Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Caramel syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">whipped cream</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the sauce, syrup and espresso in a cup, top up with milk, stir and top with whipped cream and drizzle a little syrup over the cream. Oh my.</span></p>
