<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">JURA A1 COFFEE MACHINE</span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Sharp, clear, reduced forms, elegance, compact design and the clear focus on a unique coffee result are the aspects that set the new A1 from JURA apart. The slimline one-cup machine offers everything that the heart of the coffee purist could desire.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"> It prepares coffee of a quality that otherwise only the best professional baristas would be able to achieve, and thanks to the highly innovative Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.&reg;) also produces ristretto and espresso to the same standard.</span></p>

<h3 id="pagelayout_0_pagetype_0_pagecontent_0_tabcontent_0_repTabContentBlock_conContentBlock_0_genTitle_0" class="title"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Top innovation: P.E.P.&reg; for espresso and ristretto of outstanding barista quality</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">JURA has perfected the complete brewing process for short speciality coffees. That&lsquo;s why the A1 prepares these beverages to the high standard you would expect in a coffee bar. The multi-level AromaG3&nbsp;grinder ensures that the coffee is optimally ground. It always grinds the beans fresh, quickly yet gently to preserve the aroma. The variable brewing unit has a capacity of 6 to 10 grams and ensures the ideal brewing conditions at all times. The Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.&reg;) developed by JURA optimises the extraction time. To make a ristretto or espresso, it forces the hot water through the ground coffee at short intervals. This process allows the flavours of the coffee to fully develop.</span></p>
