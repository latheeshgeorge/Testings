<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>RASPBERRY SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Raspberry Chocolate Milk</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Raspberry syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">cold milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine syrup and sauce in a&nbsp;cup, top with milk, stir and serve.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Raspberry Chai Iced Mocha</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Chai syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Raspberry syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">ice</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine syrup and sauce, pour over ice, top with milk, stir and serve.</span></p>
