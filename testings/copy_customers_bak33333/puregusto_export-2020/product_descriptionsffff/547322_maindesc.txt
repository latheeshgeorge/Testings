<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>WHITE CHOCOLATE SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Dark &amp; White Chocolate Au Lait</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">3 pumps Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird White Chocolate syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Mix the sauce and syrup then add the milk and stir well. Now that&rsquo;s a hot chocolate.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Cafe Creme Brulee</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird White Chocolate syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">cream</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">honeycomb</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the espresso and syrups in a cup, top with the milk, stir and finish with whipped cream and crumbled honeycomb. That is beyond a drink.</span></p>
