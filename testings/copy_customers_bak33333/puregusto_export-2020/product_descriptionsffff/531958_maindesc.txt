<h3 id="pagelayout_0_pagetype_0_pagecontent_0_tabcontent_0_repTabContentBlock_conContentBlock_0_genTitle_0" class="title"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">GIGA X3 Generation 2 (2019 Update) Technologies</span></h3>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">31 specialities, as well as the start screen, can be individually programmed to suit the occasion or customer. For instance, products can be renamed and arranged to the user's liking on the display. The high-performance ceramic grinder, controlled by electronic motors, guarantees quick, precise, even and consistent grinding for many years to come. As an ingenious feature, the air intake in the dual spout is electrically adjusted by a geared stepper motor. When you make a latte macchiato, the machine automatically dispenses hot milk followed, without a pause, by milk foam into the glass. This feature saves valuable time.</span></p>

<div class="block-text">
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">It's all inside the GIGA X3: The high-performance ceramic disc grinder guarantees reliable grinding results for many years which are sure to be precise, consistent and even. A top innovation provides that extra special something: A.G.A.&copy;&nbsp;(Automatic Grinder Adjustment) &ndash; the electronically controlled automatic readjustment system. The Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.&reg;) optimises the extraction time for short specialities and CLARIS Pro Smart delivers perfect water quality for a pure taste in combination with the Intelligent Water System (I.W.S.&reg;).</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">The GIGA X3 features a sophisticated height-adjustable dual spout, which finishes off trend specialities with milk and milk foam to perfection thanks to fine foam technology. The speed function ensures speciality coffees in record time. Time spent topping the machine up is reduced to a minimum thanks to the large bean container with a holding capacity of 1 kg and aroma preservation cover. What's more, the high capacity of the water tank, drip tray and coffee grounds container maximise autonomy, making the GIGA X3 ideal for mobile use too.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;" data-mce-mark="1">Increased power combined with the highest quality &ndash; that's the GIGA X3. The concise, 4.3&rdquo; high-resolution touchscreen colour display is completely intuitive. Modern graphics that are easy to understand mean that even first-time users can navigate with confidence. The dishwasher-proof drip tray can easily be removed, emptied and reinserted with just one hand. The centre of gravity is ideally located to prevent any spillages. Simple, clean, quick, convenient &ndash; this 5-star concept is incorporated down to the very last detail.</span></p>
