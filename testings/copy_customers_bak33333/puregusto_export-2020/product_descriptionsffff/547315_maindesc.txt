<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Peanut Butter Mocha</span></h3>
<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&bull; 2 pumps Sweetbird Peanut Butter syrup</span><br /><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&bull; single espresso</span><br /><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&bull; 1 scoop Zuma Original Chocolate</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Make a paste with the chocolate powder and espresso;&nbsp;add syrup, stir, top with steamed milk.</span></p>
