<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>PEACH SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Peach Schnapps Soda</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Peach syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Amaretto syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">soda (sparkling) water</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">ice</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Fill cup with ice, add the syrup and top with soda. Stir and serve.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Granita</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">4 pumps fruit-flavoured Sweetbird syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">crushed ice</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">water</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Fill&nbsp;cup with crushed ice, add 4 pumps of your favourite fruit-flavoured Sweetbird syrup and top up with water. Stir and serve.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Top tip!&nbsp;Try mixing two flavours such as pineapple and peach &ndash; just do 2 pumps of each. The combinations are endless!</span></p>
