<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Almond Mocha</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Almond syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Chocolate sauce</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double shot espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the syrup, sauce and espresso in a cup. Top with steamed milk and serve.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><span data-mce-mark="1">Top tip!</span>&nbsp;Add an extra layer of indulgence with whipped cream and a dusting of ground almonds.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Butter Pecan Latte&nbsp;</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Butterscotch syrup</span></li>

</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine espresso and syrup. Top with steamed milk. Inhale!</span></p>
