<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Lemon &amp; Ginger Hot Fruit Soothie&trade;</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">50 ml Sweetbird Lemon&nbsp;Smoothie</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps Sweetbird Gingerbread&nbsp;Syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Hot Water</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Pour smoothie into cup and add syrup. Top with hot water, stir and serve.</span></p>
