<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">BRITA PURITY WATER FILTERS</span></h1>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Brita Purity Water Filters have been developed specifically for use in the coffee industry to reduce the carbonate hardness in drinking water, thus avoiding scale deposits in the upstream appliance.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">These water filters work to remove heavy metal ions such as lead and copper, whilst also reducing cloudiness, organic impurities and chlorine that can often impair the quality and taste of the filtrate including the by-pass water.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Most applications for the Purity water filters are coffee and espresso machines.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The Brita water filters can increase machine reliability whilst also </span>being<span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"> 100% recyclable!</span></p>
