<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>Glaceau Smartwater</strong></span></h1>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Glaceau smartwater is made from British spring water which is vapour-distilled before electrolytes are added. It has a distinctive, crisp, clean taste and is produced and bottled in Morpeth, Northumberland.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Glaceau smartwater is made by using evaporated spring water then we condense the vapour and then add just the right amount of electrolytes before bottling.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>Ingredients:</strong>&nbsp;Spring Water, Electrolytes: Calcium Chloride, Magnesium Chloride, Potassium Bicarbonate.</span></p>
