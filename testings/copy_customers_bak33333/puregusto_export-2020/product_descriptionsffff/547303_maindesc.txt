<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;"><strong>CHOCOLATE SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Minty Mocha</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Mint syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 pump Sweetbird Chocolate syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine espresso and syrups in a cup and top with the milk.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie Latte</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">2 pumps of Sweetbird Chocolate syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">1 tsp of smooth peanut butter</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">double espresso</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">steamed milk</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Combine the espresso, syrup and peanut butter. Top with milk.</span></p>
