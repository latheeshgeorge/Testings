<h3><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Banana Fudge Shake</span></h3>
<div>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">2 pumps Sweetbird Banana syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">1 pump Sweetbird Caramel Fudge syrup</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">half scoop of Vanilla frapp&eacute;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">milk</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">ice</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">professional blender</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Half fill cup with ice and top up with milk. Pour into blender, add syrup and&nbsp;frapp&eacute; powder and blend until smooth. Pour back into the cup and serve with a straw.</span></p>
