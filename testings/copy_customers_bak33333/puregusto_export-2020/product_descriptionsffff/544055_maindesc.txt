<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">JURA BLUETOOTH ADAPTER</span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">Controlling your automatic coffee machine via your smartphone or tablet is now easier than ever before thanks to JURA Smart Connect, which also enables communication with smart accessories and offers brand new programming options. </span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The new "JURA Smart Connect" uses Bluetooth&reg;&nbsp;communication, based on a transmitter that is simply plugged into the coffee machine. With the app you can then wirelessly operate and program the machine and link up to various accessories.&nbsp;</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">You can use the&nbsp;<a href="https://uk.jura.com/en/support/jura-apps/jura-connect-app" target="_self"><strong>JURA Connect App</strong></a>&nbsp;to very easily access all relevant information from the preparation, day and maintenance counters via your smartphone. Easy-to-understand graphics and automatic warnings make accounting and maintenance easier than ever before for vending or operating partners.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The&nbsp;<a title="Coffee App Professional" href="https://uk.jura.com/en/support/jura-apps/jura-coffee-app-professional" target="_self"><strong>JURA Coffee App Professional</strong></a>&nbsp;conveniently brings the various setting and programming options of your JURA automatic machine to your tablet. Customise all speciality coffees to your needs, name them as you like and assign an image of your choice to them. The preparation can also be conveniently started via the tablet. The app is also ideal for use in self-service zones as the menu can be locked with a PIN code.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><br /><span style="font-size: small; font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva;">The JURA Coffee App Professional constantly keeps you informed of the machine data. Preparation, day and maintenance counters can also be accessed, as can the active prompts to take action.&nbsp;</span></p>
