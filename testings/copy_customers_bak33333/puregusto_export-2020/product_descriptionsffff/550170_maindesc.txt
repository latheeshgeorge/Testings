<h1 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">BIODEGRADABLE WATER CONES</span></h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Disposable water cones are a convenient and cost-effective solution for use with water dispensers - very popular in Schools and offices. The lightweight cone allows customers / employees / students to dispense a 4oz serving of water from a water cooler or similar device, there is minimal waste generated from this process as the cones are designed to be as lightweight as possible.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">The unique conical shape has numerous benefits; mainly that people obtaining a beverage will vend, drink then immediately dispose of the cone instead of leaving partially filled glasses on desks. The cone water cone shape means that a serving of water cannot be set down on a desk and abandoned, this makes for a tidier office and less risk of spilled drinks on equipment.</span></p>
