<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;"><strong>HONEYCOMB SYRUP RECIPE IDEAS</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Honeycomb Hot Chocolate</span></h2>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Combine 1 scoop hot chocolate with a splash of hot water</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Add 2 pumps syrup then mix to a smooth paste</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Add steamed milk, stirring as you pour</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">Chocolate Honeycomb MilkshakeHalf fill cup with ice</span></h2>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Pour milk over ice (to 1cm below the top)</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Pour contents of cup into blender jug</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Add 2 pumps syrup and 1/2 scoop frapp&eacute;</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: small;">&nbsp;Put the lid on tightly then blend until smooth</span></li>
</ul>
