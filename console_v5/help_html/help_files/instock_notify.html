How Stock Notification Request Works?
When a product is out of stock, customer can request for stock notification email for that product from product details page of the website. Stock notification is an email send to the customers whenever stock comes in place for the product.
In product add and edit page there is a tick box to decide whether or not stock notification is to be allowed for that particular product.
When customer try to order a product which is out of stock and if the stock notification is ticked for that product, then an option will be displayed to the customer in product details page to enter their email address. This email address will be recorded in the stock notification email request listing section in the console. When stock comes in place for that product, notification mail will be send to all customers who have requested for stock notification for that product. The notification request will be removed from the stock notification list in the console immediately after the notification is successfully send to the customer.
List Stock Notification Request
Stock notification requests can be searched by giving the customer email and range of dates between which the notifications are placed. Number of notifications to be shown in each of the page and also the sort order to be used can be specified. Use "Go" button to begin the search operation.
Delete operation can be done to more than one notifications at a time. Tick mark the notification to be deleted and press the "Delete" icon.
In order to view the details of product for which the notification is made, click on "Click to View Details" link displayed with each of the notifications.
When ever a stock updation occurs for products, checking is made to see whether any stock notification mails are to be send. If yes, then mails will be send.
Email template that will be used to send the mail can be managed from the "Email Template" in "Settings" menu (Settings » Email Templates).
