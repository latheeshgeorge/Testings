How Business Customers Works?
This section allows to define the business customers. Details of business should be specified such as name and address. In the edit page, options to manage departments under various business will be displayed. Customers will be mapped under the departments. The hierarchy is as, Business at the top, department under it and customers will be mapped under departments.

List Business Customers
Business can be searched by giving the business name. Number of businesses to be shown in each of the page and also the sort order to be used in search result can be specified. Use "Go" button to begin the search operation.
Use "Add" link to go the business add page. There are 2 ways to go to the edit page of an existing business. One method is by clicking the business name and another by tick marking the required business name and pressing the "Edit" icon.
Delete operation can be done to more than one businesses at a time. Tick mark the businesses to be deleted and press the "Delete" icon.
Status of businesses can be changed directly from the listing page. Tick mark the business which need status changing. Select the required status from the drop down box provided at the section "Change Status" and press the "Change" button.

Add Business
Businesses can be added through this section.
Key Fields
1.Business Name
The name for the business can be set in this text box.
2. Business Type
The type of business should be selected from this dropdown box. The types listed in the dropdown box are
a. Sole Trade
b. Partnership
c. Limited
Click on "Submit" button to save the details.

Tab: Departments under this Business
The departments under the business being edited can be managed from this section. The section list the departments added for the current business.
Click on "Add Department" button to add more departments under current business.
Click on the department name to edit a particular department details.
The hidden status of departments can be changed from this page. Tick mark the departments for which the hidden status is to be changed. Select the required status from "Change Hidden Status" drop down box and then click on "Change" button.
To delete departments, tick mark them and then click on the delete button.

Add Departments
Key fields
1. Department Name
Name of the department should be specified here. This is a compulsory field.
2. Country
The list of countries added for the site will be listed in this dropdown box. Countries can be managed from the "Country" link in the "General Settings" section (Settings » General Settings » Country).
3. State / County
By default this dropdown will not be visible. When a country is selected in the above drop down box, the list of state/county under the selected country will be listed in this drop down box. If the required state/county is not there in the drop down box, then select the option named "-Other-". This will display a new texbox where the name of the new state can be specified. State list for various countries can be managed form the "State" option in the "General Settings" section(Settings » General Settings » State).
Click on the "Save" button save the details.

Tab: Customer under this Department
The list of customers who are assigned to current department will be listed in this section.
More customers can be added to current department using the "Add Customers" button. When clicked on this button a new page will be displayed, which shows the list of customers who are not yet assigned to any of the customer departments. Tick mark the customers to be added and then click on "Assign Customers" button to make the assignment.
Customers can be unassigned from the current department by tick marking the customers and then clicking on the "Un Assing" button.
