Product Reviews
Product review section allows to manage the reviews added for various products by the site customers. The reviews added by customers will be displayed in the site only if it is approved by console admin.
List Product Reviews
The product reviews added by site customers will be listed in this section. By default this page shows the newly added product reviews. Searching can be done by specifying the product name, status of the review, author details or the date range. The number of reviews to be displayed in each page and also the sort order can be specified. Click "Go" button to do the searching.
There are 2 ways to go to the edit page of a product review. One method is by clicking the review date and another by tick marking the required review and pressing the "Edit" icon.
Delete operation can be done to more than one product review at a time. Tick mark the reviews to be deleted and press the "Delete" icon.
Review status of reviews can be changed directly from the listing page. Tick mark the review which need status changing. Select the required status from the drop down box provided at the section "Change Review Status" and press the "Change" button.
Hidden status of reviews can also be changed directly from the listing page. Tick mark the reviews which need status changing. Select the required status from the drop down box provided at the section "Change Hidden Status" and press the "Change" button.
Edit Product Review
Details of reviews can be viewed and edited from this section.
Key Fields
1. Product Name
The name of the product for which the review is posted by the customer.
2. Review Rating
The rating given to the product by the customer.
3. Review Author
The name of the customer who wrote the review.
4. Review Date
The date on which the review is posted by the customer.
5. Review Status
When a product review is posted by a customer, its status will be "New". When the review details are viewed by the console user, the status will automatically change from "New" to "Pending". If the status is set to "Approved", the review will get displayed in the read review section for respective product in the site. Once the status is set to "Approved", then no further changing is allowed to the review status.
6. Review Details
The review details posted by the customer will be displayed in this section. Console user can make any changes to these details. Click on "Save" button to save the changes.
